Introduction:

A computer is an electronic device designed to process, store, and display information.

Description:

Hardware: The physical components of a computer, such as the monitor, keyboard, and motherboard, are known as hardware. Software: Software includes the programs and operating systems that instruct the hardware on how to perform tasks.

Functionality:

Computers can perform a wide range of functions, from simple calculations to complex simulations, making them essential tools in modern life.
